# 03 Build Variable Roles

Classify all model variables and justify their roles.

Required actions:

1. Identify independent variables.
2. Identify dependent variables.
3. Identify mediators.
4. Identify moderators.
5. Identify control variables.
6. Identify higher-order constructs and dimensions.
7. For each variable, provide evidence source, role rationale, and confidence level.
8. Mark unsupported variables as not confirmed.

A variable may only enter the final model when it has a clear theoretical and empirical reason.
