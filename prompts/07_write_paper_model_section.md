# 07 Write Paper Model Section

Write a paper-ready model section based on the validated model.

Include:

1. Research background.
2. Management problem.
3. Theory gap.
4. Proposed model logic.
5. Hypothesis development.
6. Variable measurement suggestions.
7. Data and method suggestions.
8. Theoretical contribution.
9. Practical contribution.
10. Reviewer risk and revision suggestions.

Separate evidence-supported statements from proposed extensions.
